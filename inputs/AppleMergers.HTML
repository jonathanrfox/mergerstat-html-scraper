Deal Description:

Apple Inc acquired Beats Electronics LLC, a portfolio company of The Carlyle Group LP, for US$3 billion in cash and stock. The acquisition also includes Beats Music LLC, a unit of Beats Electronics LLC. The aggregate consideration is composed of US$2.6 billion in cash and US$400 million in stock. Last May 8, 2014, Apple Inc was reportedly rumored to be in negotiations to acquire Beats Electronics LLC for US$3.2 billion. The transaction would expand Apple Inc's market presence into the high-end headphones and subscription music industries. Co-founded by music industry veteran Jimmy Iovine and hip-hop producer and artist Dr. Dre, Beats Electronics LLC is located in Santa Monica, California, United States and manufactures headphones and other related devices. As part of the agreement, Jimmy Iovine and Dr. Dre will become part of Apple Inc's team. The transaction was subject to regulatory approvals. Completion of the transaction was originally expected to occur in the fourth quarter of Apple Inc's fiscal year 2014. On June 16, 2014, the deal received clearance from the Conselho Administrativo de Defesa Econômica of Brazil. On July 28, 2014, the transaction received its clearance from the European Commission. On August 1, 2014, Vivendi SA completed the sale of its 13% minority stake in Beats Electronics LLC for US$404 million. Immediately after Vivendi SA's press release, Apple Inc announced that it has officially closed the acquisition of the entire shareholding in Beats Electronics LLC.
