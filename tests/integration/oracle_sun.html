Deal Description:

Oracle Corp acquired Sun Microsystems Inc for approximately $7.1 billion in cash, or $9.50 per share. The transaction follows the failed attempts from IBM and Sun to complete their previously announced transaction. Oracle cited the Solaris technologies of Sun Microsystems Inc as a key attraction for doing the deal. In addition, Oracle Corp owns the MySQL database, which Sun had acquired in 2008.
