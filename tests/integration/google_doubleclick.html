Deal Description:

Google Inc acquired DoubleClick Inc from Hellman & Friedman LLC, JMI Inc, and management for US$3.1 billion in cash. The acquisition allows Google Inc to offer superior tools for targeting, serving, and analyzing online ads of all types, significantly benefiting customers and consumers. In addition, the acquisition strengthens Google Inc's advertising network with expanded access to publisher inventory. DoubleClick Inc is a New York-based provider of marketing technology and services with 17 offices and 15 data centers worldwide.
